Welcome, adventurer!

Dare ye enter on a realm filled with unimaginable terrors, perilous and life-threatening quests, breath-taking landscapes and immersive medieval fantasy stories? There is also plenty of mead and fun to be had!

The world needs heroes - now, more than ever!

Fight for the freedom of your descendants!

When Holdyr's last fragment, which sacrificed a piece of itself to give u Power, disappeared, the already existing Gods started a war for the highest Place of Power.
Their endless and meaningless warfare brought pain and suffering upon the mortals, who, after having enough of the undeserved pain, gathered all their forces, trained their Power, and marched forth to take down the Gods.
Several Gods have allied hemselves with the races of the Planes, others have created their own wicked and tainted Armies to fight the Planebeings.
... but, of course, in such perilous times, villains appear from under the shadows. Friend turns to foe. Heroes that fall come back twisted and malevolent.
The worldneeds heroes, now more than ever. The Stars, which proudly represent the Gods' might, shall fall!
